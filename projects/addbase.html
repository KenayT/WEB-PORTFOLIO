ADDBASE.

Merchandise Database Platform

The Project

This project aims to brand and easily market products to some audience. The project begins with defining the brand vision—covering objectives, target customers, and product categories. It then moves through the conceptual, logical, and physical models, which outline the project’s structure and workflow. The ultimate goal of the platform is to offer creators a user-friendly, efficient, and scalable system for managing merchandise, while ensuring customers enjoy a seamless shopping experience.

The Deployment

Contributed to the backend structure and database design. My focus was on mapping out the conceptual, logical, and physical data models to ensure that the creator's inventory, user data, and transactions were handled securely and could scale effortlessly as the brands grew.
